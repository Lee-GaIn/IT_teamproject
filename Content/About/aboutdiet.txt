What is diet?
Diet is a nutritional concept that refers to the total amount of food consumed by an organism (usually humans and animals) to meet the needs of survival and development. The word diet often implies the use of specific nutrients for health or weight management reasons (with two factors involved). Although humans are carnivores, each culture and each person holds some different preferences in eating. This could be due to personal tastes or ethical reasons (the morality of eating meat). Diet choices can be more or less healthy.
Completed nutrition mode requires eating, drinking and absorbing essential vitamins, minerals, amino acids from proteins and essential fat acids from foods containing fats, as well as food energy in the form of carbohydrates, proteins, and fats. Eating habits and choices play an important role in quality of life, health and longevity.
Why diet is an important factor for our health?
. Weight Loss. One of the main reasons people eat a healthy diet is to maintain a healthy weight or to lose weight. 
. Heart Health. 
. Strong bones and teeth.

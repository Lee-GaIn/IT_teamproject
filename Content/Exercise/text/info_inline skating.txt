Inline skating is a type of roller skate. Surprisingly, it is reported that inline skates were developed by a Russian athlete to use substitutes of ice skates, specifically the athlete used modern style inline skates to prepare long track speed skating in the Olympic. The design of inline skates is various depending on the purpose. For example, inline skate for off-road skating has the largest wheel size, whereas the smallest wheel is included in aggressive skating. Therefore, people who prefer to attempt inline skating need to consider the design of inline skates to purchase a proper product. The sports is recommended for most beginners. They possibly can find several club or exercise program related to inline skating.

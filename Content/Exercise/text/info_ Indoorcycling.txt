indoor cycling

In recent days, indoor cycling and spinning have drawn attention as an exercise for people who have hectic schedules in daily life. The reason for this is that indoor cycling do not require numerous spaces. Traditional cycling asks massive area to ride, whereas only the room for indoor cycle machine is essential elements to enjoy indoor cycling. In addition, some tools like helmets and knee guard might be not necessary for indoor cycling. The sports also is not impacted weather. In conclusion, indoor cycling eliminates many barriers which disturb to do exercise so it may be an alternative for individuals who are struggling with do exercise regularly due to several conditions. 

- burning calories: 561
*The standard condition : A person who is 180cm and 80kg do exercise for 1 hour.

- required equipment: indoor cycling bikes

- Advantage

 1. It will probably be efficient method to burn fat.

 2. Strength might increase.

 3. Heart and lugs will be strengthened


- Disadvantage

 1. The cost for initial facilitate might be unaffordable

 2. It is easy to feel boring due to the monotonous environment.

 3. It is possible that the machine generates intolerable noise.

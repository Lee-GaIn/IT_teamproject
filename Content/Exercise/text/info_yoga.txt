yoga 

Yoga is an exercise well-known that it affects mental and physical health at the same time. In the report results published by the India government, it is presented that yoga concentrates on harmony between body and mind further human and nature. The origin of yoga started thousands of years ago even before the first religion was created. Based on the report, it was spread in the world by some sages of Adiyogi who is the initiator of yoga. As a result, yoga has become consistently beloved exercise until now.  Considering individuals can do yoga without any tools, it may be recommended to beginners who want to make small steps into exercise.

- advantages
 1.Yoga might affect improving back pain and blood sugar level.
 2. It helps to increase flexibility.
 3. Stress and worries will possibly be released

- disadvantages

 1. It may be inappropriate for several people, especially the elders.
 2. Some movements and poses need numerous experiences.
 3. It is hard to do yoga without suitable guidance from trainers

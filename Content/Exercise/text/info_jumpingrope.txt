 Jumping rope is one of the HIIT exercises which impact efficiently on decreasing belly fat and enhance muscles. For instance, 10 minutes of skipping rope is approximately equivalent to running an 8-minute-mile based on the <a href="https://www.sciencedaily.com/">Science Daily</a>. There are many technics of jumping rope like alternate foot jump, sid swing and criss-cross depends on times of the jumping and the number of used foot.  Although it is usually regarded as a warm-up exercise for some people, jumping rope will possibly be useful for individual who want to settle new exercise habit. Most people can buy jump rope on the internet or local supermarket or stationary. Thus, skipping rope might be suggested as the first exercise for beginners.


- burning calories: 818
*The standard condition : A person who is 180cm and 80kg do exercise for 1 hour.

- required equipment: jump rope
 

- health benefits:

 1. It helps to improve coordination
 2. Skipping rope contributes to declining of foot and ankle injuries
 3. Enhanced bone density might result from the jumping rope.

- disadvantages:

Jumping ropes is an unsuitable exercise for particular people who have any kind of heart disease, high blood pressure, asthma and some problems associated with bones.

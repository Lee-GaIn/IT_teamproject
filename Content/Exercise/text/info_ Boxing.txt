Boxing is a traditional and representative combat sports. The origin of boxing was discovered in Egypt based on the

the boxing was presented to the ancient Olympic Games by the Greeks and become popular sports in global. The techniques of boxing are compromised many elements such as stance, punches and defence so it might be crucial to accustom to basic movement. Boxing is also separated by the style of a strategic approach. The major types of boxing style are an outside fighter, usually called boxer, a brawler and inside fighter.

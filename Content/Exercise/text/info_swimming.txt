 The definition of swimming is moving through water by using the entire body. An individual can naturally float on the water without any efforts, but they have to move their legs and arms constantly to shift to another place. There are many movements of swimming such as arm stroke, breaststroke and side stroke according to 
<a href="https://troopleader.scouting.org/basic-swimming-strokes/"> troopleader.scouting.org </a>. Since each movement has a different level and required a particular posture and breath method it is expected to register a swimming program near the sports centre than learn by yourself. Moreover, many tools such as kickboards and hand paddles might be essential for starters to compensate for the lack of swimming skills.


- burning calories: 491
*The standard condition : A person who is 180cm and 80kg do exercise for 1 hour.

- required equipment: Swimsuit and goggles.

- health benefits:
 1. It will possibly contribute to alleviating stress.
 2. Balance and posture might enhance.
 3. It helps to improve flexibility.

https://www.betterhealth.vic.gov.au/health/healthyliving/swimming-health-benefits

- disadvantages:

 1. Chemicals in the swimming pools like chlorine may affect an individual's body.
 2. Risk of drowning may exist.
 3. Common injuries will be expected. 

https://swimcompetitive.com/swimming-guides/disadvantages-of-swimming/

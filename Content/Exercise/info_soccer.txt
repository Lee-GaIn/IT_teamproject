- definition of exercise:  
	Soccer, sometimes named football depends on the country, is not only well-known sports in the world but also played continuously regardless of the nationality. <a href="https://www.huffpost.com/entry/soccer-worlds-best-sport_n_5248061#:~:text=According%20to%20FIFA's%20most%20recent,2.">FIFA's survey</a>reported there are 265 millions soccer players around the world. Soccer is a team sport including eleven members and ball is a necessary tool to play it. The winners of the game are determined when they get the higher points by moving the ball beyond the goal line opposed from the team. It means that the players need to consider offences to score as well as defence to block the opposite team. In this process, people can grasp leaderships and teamwork.

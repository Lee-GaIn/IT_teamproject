
- explain : 
Taekwondo is a martial art in Korea. It stemmed from Tae Kyon which is another martial arts started about 37BC. In 2000, Taekwondo became one of the Olympic events. The typical traits of Taekwondo basically consist of punches, blocks and kicks according to 
<a href="http://www.taekwondoclub.london/history-of-taekwondo">taekwondoclub.london</a>. However, it is possible having different styles and even different names for same movements based on the associations related to Taekwondo such as ATA, ITF, GTF and WT so it may be significant to understand which association's rules do practice follow when learning Taekwondo.
